Our Course


Warm-up Zone:

Please stay in the warm-up lane, arrows, after launching and make your way into the warm-up area in the basin.After finishing the race, please go into the warm-up lane and make your way to the dock.

Start:

Five minutes before your race start time, please move up to the starting area from the warm-up zone. Get alignment, and prepare to start. This 500 meters ends just after the bridge, and both boats are able to fit side-by-side so long as they stay in their lanes.

500 meters:

500 meters into the course, the river starts to bend and it is needed for boats to remain in their lanes as they round this turn.

1000 meters:

In the third 500, boats are coming out of the bend in the river, and onto the final straightaway. Boats should align their points to the two orange flags at the bridge on the end of the straight.

1500 meters:

In the final 500, boats should remain straight until the finish, making sure not to stray into the warm-up lane. A horn will sound when each boat crosses the finish just before the bridge.
